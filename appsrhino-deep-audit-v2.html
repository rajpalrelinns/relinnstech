Critical Issues — Fix Before Anything Else

BLOCKER
🔴

BLOCKER 1 — Identical Alt Text on Every Single Image

All images across the site use alt="Custom mobile app development company". This is keyword stuffing in Google's eyes, wastes image SEO entirely, and harms accessibility. Google's crawlers see zero semantic differentiation between 100+ images. This single issue is actively suppressing image-search traffic and may trigger a spam signal for repetitive anchor-equivalent usage.

Dev Time: 4hrsTraffic Impact: +15-25%Fix: Unique descriptive alt per image
🔴

BLOCKER 2 — Brand Split: "AppsRhino" vs "Relinns Technologies"

The official Joget partner page lives at relinns.com/joget-partner. The main site is appsrhino.com. Video testimonials mention "Relinns Technologies." This creates two separate entity graphs in Google's Knowledge Graph and AI systems. Backlinks, citations, and authority are split between two domains. This is killing domain authority consolidation and confusing both Google's entity recognition and LLM citation systems.

Fix Time: 1 weekSEO Impact: Very HighFix: Consolidate to one primary domain + 301 redirects
🔴

BLOCKER 3 — All Blog Post Cards Show Same Generic Placeholder Image

Every blog post on the listing page loads "appsrhinoGenericImage.png". Google Image Search, social sharing previews (OG images), and AI crawlers all receive zero contextual signal about post content. CTR from social shares drops 30-40% with generic images. Content that gets cited by AI systems needs clear topic-specific imagery to reinforce topical relevance.

Fix: Bulk create OG imagesCTR Impact: +30-40%Tool: Canva batch or Figma
🟡

CRITICAL 4 — Zero Pricing Transparency Across All Products

None of the 14 product/service pages show pricing, ranges, or starting points. Competitors like Appinventiv, Hyperlink InfoSystem, and Chop Dawg show "$25-49/hour" and "$15,000-$50,000 typical project." AI systems (Perplexity, ChatGPT) heavily cite pages with pricing data when users ask "how much does app development cost." Missing pricing = missing AI citations for highest-intent queries.

🟡

CRITICAL 5 — No Author Attribution on Any Blog Post

All published blogs on appsrhino.com lack author names, photos, or bios. Per Google's Helpful Content Update (2023-2024) and E-E-A-T guidelines, anonymous content ranks lower — especially for YMYL-adjacent topics like healthcare apps, fintech, compliance. Competitors Appinventiv and Arkenea have detailed author profiles with credentials, LinkedIn links, and article counts.

🟡

CRITICAL 6 — No Dedicated Pages for 8 Products (SEO Traffic Leak)

FlutterFlow App Development, Telemedicine App Development, Cannabis App Development, Joget Consultancy, Joget Support, Joget Implementation, and Custom Scalable Apps — none have unique, optimized landing pages with proper H1, meta, schema, and content depth. Traffic for these high-value search terms is either lost or poorly targeted.

Issues Directly Hurting Your Traffic — Right Now

HIGH IMPACT

Google Images drives 22% of all web searches. Identical alt text means AppsRhino captures nearly zero image search traffic. Competitors with unique, descriptive alt text rank for "[product] app screenshot," "[service] development process," etc.

FAQ schema enables Google to show expanded Q&A directly in SERPs (rich snippets), significantly increasing CTR (up to +20-30%) and AI Overview citations. Service pages and blog posts have zero structured Q&A — competitors using FAQPage schema are stealing featured snippets AppsRhino could own.
